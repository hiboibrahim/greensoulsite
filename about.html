About Us

At Green Soul, our initiatives are designed to drive meaningful change and support personal and community growth. Through the creation of community gardens, we provide individuals with the opportunity to cultivate their own food, engage in sustainable practices, and connect with others, fostering a strong sense of community. Our educational workshops empower participants with valuable knowledge and skills on topics ranging from gardening and nutrition to mental health and wellness, equipping them to take proactive steps in their lives. We also focus on building supportive networks, offering opportunities for people to forge connections and find mentorship, which helps combat isolation and nurtures a sense of belonging. Additionally, we advocate for social inclusion and work towards systemic change by partnering with local organizations and stakeholders. The positive impact of our work is evident in the enhanced self-confidence, improved well-being, and strengthened community bonds reported by our participants, highlighting our commitment to transforming lives and promoting a more equitable society.

Community Activities

Green Soul are committed to promoting equity, well-being, and environmental stewardship through collaborative and inclusive activities.

Sip and Social Groups: Foster community connections through casual meet-ups and discussions.

Family Activities: Gardening and Forest School: Engage families in hands-on environmental education and outdoor activities.

Food and Nutrition Support: Whole-Foods Co-operative: Provide access to affordable, fresh, and dry goods.

Creative and Environmental Engagement: Arts and Crafts Sessions to encourage creativity and self-expression.

Climate Conversations: Facilitate discussions on environmental issues and sustainability.
