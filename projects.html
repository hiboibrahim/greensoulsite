Nourishing Communities and Building Connections

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to fostering well-being and community through thoughtful, impactful projects. Our initiatives are designed to address various aspects of food security and community cohesion, ensuring that everyone has access to nutritious food and a supportive network. Building ttronger communities, one meal at a time.

Food Co-operative

Green Soul's Food Cooperative, held fortnightly at the Grangetown Hub, is a vibrant and inclusive community initiative designed to promote sustainable living, healthy eating, and local food systems. Operating every other Saturday from 12 PM to 3 PM, the cooperative offers a platform for residents to access fresh, locally-sourced produce, engage with one another, and participate in the collaborative spirit of the food-sharing movement.

Our Food Cooperative at Grangetown Hub aims to build community by creating a shared space for sourcing, preparing, and enjoying food. We prioritize local and seasonal produce to support regional farmers and reduce environmental impact, while promoting sustainable practices such as minimal packaging and waste reduction. The cooperative fosters connections among individuals and families, providing a platform for sharing recipes and building relationships. Additionally, we work to ensure that nutritious, high-quality food is accessible to everyone, regardless of financial situation, contributing to a more equitable and engaged community.

This holistic approach not only addresses hunger but also empowers participants with knowledge and skills to make healthier food choices, ultimately contributing to their overall well-being and self-sufficiency.
