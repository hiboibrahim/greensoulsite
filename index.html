Green Soul is a grassroots community organisation centred on social inclusion. We actively seek to challenge urban inequalities and work with disadvantaged groups where people are marginalised through poverty or lack of opportunity. Through our growing projects we aim to have a transformational impact on people’s physical and mental health, isolation and loneliness. Our ‘asset-based’ approach means we choose not to focus on ill-health or problems, but rather to inspire and nurture people, realising their gifts and skills. As community gardens grow, so do the people. We want to see lives transformed through community gardening, becoming connected, supportive, healthier, empowered and joyful.

What We Do

At Green Soul, we are dedicated to fostering vibrant, inclusive communities through the power of gardening and collaboration. Our initiatives are designed to uplift and support individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds by creating opportunities for connection, growth, and well-being.

Community Gardening: We transform urban spaces into thriving gardens where individuals and families can come together, cultivate plants, and build meaningful relationships. Our gardens serve as a hub for learning, sharing, and personal development, offering a hands-on approach to improving physical health and mental well-being.

Support and Empowerment: Our programs are centered around nurturing individual strengths and talents. We offer workshops, training, and resources that empower participants to take charge of their own growth and contribute positively to their communities.

Social Inclusion: By focusing on social inclusion, we work to bridge gaps and reduce isolation among marginalized groups. Our activities are designed to create welcoming environments where everyone feels valued and supported, fostering a sense of belonging and community.

Impact and Transformation: We measure our success by the positive changes in our participants' lives. From reducing loneliness to enhancing self-esteem and resilience, our goal is to facilitate transformative experiences that lead to healthier, more connected, and empowered individuals.

Through our diverse range of projects and initiatives, Green Soul is committed to creating lasting, positive change, one garden at a time.

Our Goals

By focusing on nurturing personal strengths and building connections, we seek to improve physical and mental health, reduce isolation, and empower individuals to actively contribute to their communities. Our ultimate aspiration is to see a ripple effect of positive change, where the benefits of community gardening extend beyond the gardens themselves, enhancing the overall well-being and resilience of the people we serve.

Equity and Inclusion

Empowering all voices through inclusive and accessible artistic expression.

Sustainability and Health

Combating food insecurity and promoting sustainable health for people and the planet.

Community Empowerment

Strengthening communities and well-being through support and Education.

Innovation and Collaboration

We develop creative solutions to pressing social issues, enhancing our impact and driving positive change.
